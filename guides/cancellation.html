Cancelling Your Plan

What happens to your number and your service

We're sorry to see you go. Here's exactly what happens — step by step — so there are no surprises.
What Happens When You Cancel
1
Your subscription ends
Your billing stops on the date you choose. No further charges will be made to your card.
2
Call forwarding is deactivated
We'll guide you to turn off forwarding with one simple code. Your calls go back to ringing your phone directly.
3
Your number stays reserved for 30 days
Your dedicated number is not given to anyone else immediately — we keep it reserved just for you.
4
After 30 days, the number may be reassigned
If you don't return within 30 days, the number becomes available for a new client.
ℹ Good to Know
If you cancel and come back within 30 days, we'll do our best to give you back the same number so your existing customers don't notice any change.
Why We Wait 30 Days
Your past customers may still have this number saved in their phone. Waiting avoids confusion if they try to contact you again.
It gives you time to make sure call forwarding is properly switched off on your own phone.
It protects your business identity — no other tradesperson will receive messages meant for you.
One Last Step
##61#
Please dial this code to deactivate call forwarding after cancelling. It only takes 10 seconds.
